"Rich Dad Poor Dad" by Robert Kiyosaki is a personal finance book that compares the lessons he learned from his two "dads" — his real (poor) dad and his best friend’s (rich) dad. The book teaches how to achieve financial independence through investing, owning businesses, and understanding money — not just working for a salary. Its main idea: The rich make money work for them, while the poor and middle class work for money.
